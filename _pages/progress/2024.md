<A HREF="/assets/pdf/OptimizingCostRecoveryForAgenciesAndProviders.pdf">Cost Recovery Best Practices</A>
GSA engaged with Agencies, Providers, and industry (through public comment) to establish community-wide agreement on baseline best practices that promote optimal cost recovery models. In particular, aligning to agency budget lifecycle and supporting the funding of both current operations and the planning needed for future modernizations.
