            <ol class="phases">
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Assesment</h4>
                    Build a vision and business case for migration
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Readiness</h4>
                    Prepare the customer for the modernization effort and define business, functional and technical requirements
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Selection</h4>
                    Conduct due diligence to select the provider based on business requirements and desired target and end state
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Engagement</h4>
                    Conduct detailed planning through a fit-gap analysis and finalize the migration and O&M approach and costs
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Migration</h4>
                    Configure, test and deploy the new system, concept of operations, and workforce design
                </li>
                <li>
                    <h4 class="text-heavy">Operations</h4>
                    Deliver services and conduct continuous improvement
                </li>
            </ol>
        </div>
    </div>
