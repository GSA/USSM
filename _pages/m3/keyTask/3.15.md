    responsibleParty: shared
  - text: Develop gap solutions for contact center gaps and adjust estimated cost of services
    responsibleParty: shared
  - text: Document Integrated Contact Center Strategy to implement changes to contact center processes, roles, and capacity for customer and provider
    responsibleParty: shared
  - text: Report updates in governance meetings and Status Reports/Dashboards
    responsibleParty: shared
